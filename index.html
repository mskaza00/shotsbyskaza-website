About

Behind The Lens

I'm Matthew Skaza, a 17-year-old photographer based in Western Massachusetts, specifically the Chicopee area. I specialize in sports photography while also capturing portraits and other special moments. With over two years of experience behind the camera, I enjoy creating images that tell meaningful stories and preserve memories that matter to people.

When I'm not taking photos, I'm a student-athlete at Chicopee Comprehensive High School and a football captain. My experience as an athlete gives me a unique perspective when capturing sports, allowing me to understand the emotion, dedication, and intensity behind every moment. From rivalry games and state championships to couples shoots and unforgettable events, I use my camera to capture moments that people can look back on for years to come.
